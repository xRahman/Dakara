Convert to Drawing
-------------------

https://effectphoto.blogspot.com/2015/08/turn-photo-into-sketch-using-gimp.html

1) Duplicate layer

2) Change its blend mode to Dodge

3) Invert its color

4) Go to Filters > Distorts > Value Propagate

  Select Mode: More black (smaller value)
  Leave other values

5) Repeat step 4 (Ctrl + F) until you are happy with the result

6) Desaturate both layers

Obarvení
----------

https://graphicdesign.stackexchange.com/questions/111121/how-do-i-change-the-color-of-a-png-image-in-gimp


1) Set the alpha lock for the layer (checkerboard icon in the "Lock" line at the top of the layers list)

2) Bucket-fill the layer (without any selection) with color (3c1e00)
